2023 年 5 月 9 日

Liquid Physics V2 Progress

液体物理学 V2 进展

As I've mentioned before I am currently working on liquid physics V2. The goal is to get those high detailed fluid simulations like in the Minecraft animation videos. The old algorithm for it basically created a 3D model from spheres in the world which was very limitting. It stopped working at around 1.000 spheres. To achieve high resolution quality it must work with up to 100.000 spheres or more. With the new method that should easily work now BUT the compatibility with shaders is much harder which will require a lot of additional work as well. The last and final step is the physics simulation itself. PhysX 4.1 (the physics engine I use) didn't support a real fluid simulation which meant I had to use spheres and crudely approximate fluid behaviour. This also meant that it lagged a lot with 5.000+ spheres in the world. PhysX 5.1 recently got released and as some of you are aware the physics mod version for Minecraft 1.20 uses PhysX 5.1. This version supports real fluid dynamics although the Java wrapper for it isn't done yet which will also take some time.

The goal of the liquid physics is to replace the entirety of water in Minecraft (only client side and with the help of the ocean physics). So basically small sources of water will have extreme high quality simulations and big bodies of water will use the ocean physics. However as a starting point I would like to only have the liquid physics as a sort of cinematic feature since the implementation into the vanilla Minecraft gameplay will be very complex as well.

The video above demonstrates the amount of spheres being rendered (7k+) without any use of fancy shaders (for now).

正如我之前提到的，我目前正在开发液体物理 V2。我们的目标是获得像 Minecraft 动画视频中那样高精细的流体模拟。旧版算法基本上是通过世界中的球体来创建 3D 模型，这非常有局限性。大约 1.000 个球体时，它就停止工作了。要实现高分辨率质量，必须使用多达 100.000 个球体或更多。新方法现在应该可以轻松实现，但与着色器的兼容性要困难得多，这也需要大量的额外工作。最后也是最后一步是物理模拟本身。PhysX 4.1（我使用的物理引擎）不支持真正的流体模拟，这意味着我不得不使用球体并粗略地近似流体行为。这也意味着，当世界上有 5000 多个球体时，它的运行会非常滞后。最近，PhysX 5.1 发布了，正如你们中的一些人所知，Minecraft 1.20 的物理 MOD 版本使用了 PhysX 5.1。该版本支持真正的流体动力学，不过其 Java 封装还未完成，这也需要一些时间。

液体物理的目标是取代整个威廉与大陆中的水（仅在客户端和海洋物理的帮助下）。因此，基本上小水源将有极高质量的模拟，大水体将使用海洋物理。不过，作为一个起点，我希望只将液体物理特性作为一种电影功能，因为在《威廉与魔法》游戏中的实现也将非常复杂。

2023 年 4 月 24 日

Physics Mod Pro is now Free!

物理模组专业版现已免费！

I am pleased to inform everyone that, in response to Mojang's request, my mod is now available for free.

While this decision was prompted by Mojang's request, I am optimistic about the future and hopeful for the continued support from the Minecraft community. Your passion, feedback, and engagement have been invaluable to the development of my mod, and I am grateful for the opportunity to share it with all of you.

I am excited about the possibilities that lie ahead, and I look forward to continuing this journey with all of you. Thank you for your ongoing support, and let's work together to create an even more vibrant Physics Mod than ever before!

M1/M2 Mac support is only available with the new PhysX engine in the 23w16a snapshot!

Installation Instructions/Tested Setups: https://minecraftphysicsmod.com/installation

Known Mod/MacOS compatibility issues: https://pastebin.com/iYFLiRMH

Join our Discord community: https://discord.gg/VsNs9xP

Iris Compatibility: Minecraft 1.16.5/1.17.1 requires Iris 1.4.2 or lower.

IMPORTANT Ocean Physics Information: Only use a maximum of 12 chunk render distance. Anything higher could result in big lag spikes.

**Ocean Physics Supported Shaders: https://pastebin.com/MttZQncu

Smoke Physics Information: Minecraft particles have to be enabled for it to work! The further away you are from smoke sources the less particles spawn. Temporal Anti-Aliasing (or TAA) can cause issues with the smoke so disabling it will eliminate some visual artifacts. Not all shaders are supported (best results that I've got are with BSL shaders).

Snow Physics Information: High render distances on slow CPUs might result in delayed snow tracks.

Liquid Physics Information: https://pastebin.com/aZc42U5T

我对未来的可能性感到兴奋，并期待着与大家一起继续这段旅程。感谢你们一直以来的支持，让我们共同努力，创造一个比以往更加生机勃勃的物理模组！

M1/M2 Mac 支持仅适用于 23w16a 快照中的新 PhysX 引擎！

安装说明/测试设置： https://minecraftphysicsmod.com/installation

已知模组/MacOS 兼容性问题： https://pastebin.com/iYFLiRMH

加入我们的 Discord 社区： https://discord.gg/VsNs9xP

Iris 兼容性： Minecraft 1.16.5/1.17.1 需要 Iris 1.4.2 或更低版本。

重要的海洋物理信息： 最多只能使用 12 块的渲染距离。任何更高的距离都可能导致严重的延迟。

**支持海洋物理着色器： https://pastebin.com/MttZQncu

烟雾物理信息： Minecraft 粒子必须启用才能工作！离烟雾源越远，产生的粒子越少。时间抗锯齿（或 TAA）会导致烟雾出现问题，因此禁用它可以消除一些视觉伪影。并非所有着色器都支持（我使用 BSL 着色器得到的效果最好）。

雪地物理信息： 在慢速 CPU 上的高渲染距离可能会导致雪迹延迟。
